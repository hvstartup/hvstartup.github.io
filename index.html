HV Startup Communities

About Us

HV Startup is a community of influencers and catalysts, engineers and educators, designers and developers, manufacturers and entrepreneurs who reside in the Hudson Valley, and are committed to actively facilitating economic development, innovation and entrepreneurial outcomes in this region.

Team

While HV Startup is defined by a broader community, we see value to a core set of leaders and committes that can be used to tackle specific challenges or create momentum around specific initiatives of interest. The initial leadership group consists of the following individuals, each of whom currently leads or manages a group or initiative within the Hudson Valley startup ecosystem
